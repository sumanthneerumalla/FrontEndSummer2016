Definition lists

The definition list is created with the dl element and usually consists of a series of terms and definition. The list contains dt and dd elements. dt elements contain the term being defined, and the dd elements contain the term being defined. It is possible to have more than one term per definition.

Sashimi
Sliced raw fish, a type of sushi
Scale
A device used to meausre the weight of an ingredient
Procrastinate
To put off what you can easily do now, for tomorrow.

And finally, the last type of list: The Nested List

It is possible to put lists inside of lists. Nested elements are typically just indented further than the parent list item.
